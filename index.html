Scam Threat

Warning: You could have just encountered a potential scam!

It's crucial to be cautious online and recognize the signs of scams. Scammers often use deceptive tactics to trick individuals into providing personal information or financial details.

Common Signs of Scams:

  • Unsolicited emails asking for personal or financial information.
  • Requests for payment via unconventional methods (gift cards, wire transfers).
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers or prizes that require upfront payment.
  • Mismatched or suspicious URLs.

What to Do:

If you suspect a scam:

  • Do not provide personal or financial information.
  • Verify the legitimacy of the website or communication through official channels.
  • Report scams to the appropriate authorities.

Stay informed and share this information with friends and family to help protect everyone from online scams.
